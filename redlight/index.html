Amsterdam's Guides was established in 2016 with a focus on offering truly immersive, personalized walking tours of hidden gems, untold stories, and insider knowledge that you won't find in any guidebook. Tour description

Discover the fascinating story of Amsterdam with Amsterdam's expert guides. Together with your super-local guide, you will uncover hidden gems, stroll through charming canals, and learn about the city's rich history, from its Golden Age to modern-day wonders. Explore iconic landmarks like the Red Light District and the Old Church, while hearing captivating tales about Amsterdam's port and its haunted history of prostitution. You'll experience the heart of Amsterdam like never before.

We combine beautiful sightseeing with exciting storytelling and cover everything you need to know about Amsterdam in one tour: history, modern life, culture, art, fun facts, and personal anecdotes...
